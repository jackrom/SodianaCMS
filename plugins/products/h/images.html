If you upload images here, they can be shown in the frontend by adding codes such as {{PRODUCTS_IMAGE}} (to show the default image) or {{PRODUCTS_IMAGES}} (to show clickable thumbnails of all images) to the template in this product's Product Type.

To upload an image, use the "Browse..." button to find the image[s] you want to upload. In modern browsers you can upload more than one at a time.

To delete an image, tick the checkbox under the image to verify that you mean it, then click "delete".

To mark an image as the default product image, click "set default".
